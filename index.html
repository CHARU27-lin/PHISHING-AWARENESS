🎣 What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers pretend to be trusted organizations to steal passwords, OTPs, and personal information.


Attackers commonly use fake emails, websites, SMS messages, and phone calls.

⚠️ Phishing is one of the biggest causes of cyber security breaches worldwide.

🔍 Types of Phishing

Email Phishing

Fake emails pretending to be from trusted services.

Spear Phishing

Targeted attacks against specific users.

Whaling

Attacks targeting executives and CEOs.

Smishing

SMS based phishing attacks.

🛡️ How to Recognize Phishing

🔗 Check URLs Carefully

Real:

https://www.sbi.co.in

Fake:

https://sbi-login-security.com

📧 Verify Sender Email

Real:

support@google.com

Fake:

support@google-helpdesk.com

🌍 Real World Examples

❌ Fake SBI Email
